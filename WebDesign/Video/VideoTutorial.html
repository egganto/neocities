Video Embedding Tutorial

Hello! Let's learn how to embed a video from YouTube into your website. We'll go through it together step-by-step.

Step 1

Go to your YouTube video and click the share button.

Step 2

Now click the Embed button

Step 3

Change the preferences/permissions however you like, and AFTER doing so copy the provided code

Step 4

Go back to your code, and paste the code you copied for the Embed where you want it to be



And that's it! You now have an embedded YouTube video on your website.
