Takasaki Festival

This year's Hanabi Taikai at Takasaki is also going to have a matsuri. You can come enjoy watching the fireworks and setting picnics at the park before and during the event. There will be several pop-up stores and stands where you can buy matsuri items, food, accessories, etc. Expect the event place to be crowded. Masks are required.
Firecrackers are also sold at the place of event. On September 3, 2020, the firework show will begin at 8 PM.

general info

The festivals and firework shows are generally held on Saturdays (more info in the official website), ready from 6 PM.
